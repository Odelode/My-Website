Scott Pilgrim VS The World Review

Review

Scott Pilgrim VS The World has consistently been my favorite movie for several years. It has an amazing score made up of rock music, and the original songs made for the movie are absoloutely amazing. The colors are so vibrant and I love it, the cinematography is great, the comic book vibe is also so incredible, there's so much I can talk about and I love every single thing about it (except Scott Pilgrim as a human not a character). For all the reasons I've stated I'm making this a 10/10.
